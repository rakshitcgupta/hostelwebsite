Events
Main events

Freshers Party

An Amazing Experience for the juniors who are just admitted to S.P.I.T. The motive of this event is to break the ice between the senior and the junior students. Inter hostel dance and sports competitions are the key attractions of this event.

Treasure Hunt

This event was aimed to make the freshers familiar with the campus and the hostel premises. The winning teams will be awarded with cash prize and goodies. A list of 10 clues related to technical and non technical questions are to solved to reach till the final destination.

Open Mic

The motive of this event is to make the residents more confident and share their views on ttopics which they feel are revelant in the society.Stand up comedy and poetry are the key highlights of this event.

Food challenge

One of the most popular event of the hostel where the hostelites run food stalls for an entire day and earn the profits by selling their own cooked food.

Sardar Patel Premier League-Cricket

Annual Cricket tournament which involves auctions for hostelites and forms 6 teams which compete in knockout stages for the SPPL cup every year.

Football Tournament

Another sports extravaganza which is organised by the sports council of the hostel.5 knockout stages of the competition which decide the football champs of the hostel.

Tug of War

This event tests the strength of the teams which participate in this competition. A team of 8 players compete through the various round to win this event.

Farewell Party

That time of the year when the B.E. students passout and are given an awesome farewell by all the junior students. Definately the most emotional moment in the life of any engineering student.
